Hi there, I'm Tommy Armstrong and I'm currently
building a career in tech.

I'm from Olympia, Washington and recently completed a career change to tech. The career change culminated with a job at SAS Institute and recently graduating from Oregon State University's post-bacc BS Computer Science degree. Prior to that I graduated with degrees in Biochemistry, Chemistry and Mathematics from the University of Washington. I've always had an affinity for numbers, data and programming which is one of the reasons why I switched to tech. It allows me to build a career out of that affinity. I currently reside in Raleigh, NC having moved here from the west coast in June of 2020.


I recently graduated from my Computer Science degree with a 4.0 GPA in March of 2021. Throughout this degree I realized I really enjoy working with web frameworks. Especially those that are Python based like Django and Flask. My first job in tech has been as a technical consultant at SAS Institute Inc. I get to build analytical software solutions for clients and provide avenues to meaningful data analysis and visualization.


In my spare time I love getting outdoors. I'm a pretty competitive guy so I love playing sports, especially soccer and volleyball. I also like to get out in nature, spending much of the spring and summer hiking. I even summited Mt. Rainier the summer of 2017. I'm currently looking to get more involved with running and hope to complete my first half-marathon and subsequent marathon in the near future.
